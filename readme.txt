To play the game, run "game.py" this is where all of the travelling takes place. 

Player stats at the end of the game are stored in "data.txt"

All of the information about the different locations in the game are located in "locations.py"

There are two narratives presented at different points in the gameplay. They are "warning.txt" and "field.txt" 

There is a dictionary storing the player's stats (health, stamina, and skill) in the "stats.py" file.
